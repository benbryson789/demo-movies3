Science Fiction Movies

Science Fiction is a genre of fiction of futuristic stories about science and technology. The stories entail true and fictitious laws or theories of science. A human character discusses the effect new discoveries, happenings and scientific developments will have on us in the future. The events of Science fiction films usually occur in the future, in space, on a different world, or in a different universe or dimension.

The Matrix film tells the story of responsive machines to calm the human population, while the heat and electrical activity from their bodies are extracted and used as an energy source. Groups of individuals discover the truth and launch a rebellion against the machines.

Characters:

  • Keanu Reeves

Description:

The Avatar film occurs in the 22nd century when humans are colonizing a moon to mine the mineral unobtanium used for superconductors. The colonial expansion threatens the existence of the indigenous, humanoid population. A genetically engineered native body with the mind of a remote human is used to socialize with the locals.

Characters:

  • Sam Worthington
  • Zoe Saldana
  • Stephen Lang
  • Sigourney Weaver
